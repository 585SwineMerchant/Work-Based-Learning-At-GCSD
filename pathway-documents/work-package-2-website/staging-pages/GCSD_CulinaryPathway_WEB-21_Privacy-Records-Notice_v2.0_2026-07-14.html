Privacy and Records

Privacy and Records Notice

The public site may explain the program. It should not collect or expose student, employer, placement, evaluation, hours, agreement, incident, or Passport records.

Current public practice

What this public site collects

No public forms

The staged pages do not collect submissions, upload files, or receive completed records.

No student progress records

Passport records, Employability Profiles, grades, evaluations, and individual evidence are not public website content.

No employer records

Site reviews, agreements, training plans, hours, evaluations, incidents, and corrective actions require restricted district handling.

Restricted examples

What must never be submitted here

  • Completed Passport records or Employability Profiles.
  • Student schedules, accommodations, grades, reflections, evaluations, or discipline information.
  • Employer agreements, site reviews, training plans, hours logs, incident reports, or corrective action records.
  • Private contact details, medical information, payment information, or transportation records.
Future systems

When this notice must change

If the site later connects a form, analytics service, calendar, downloadable packet, or authenticated participant area, this notice must be reviewed and updated before publication.
