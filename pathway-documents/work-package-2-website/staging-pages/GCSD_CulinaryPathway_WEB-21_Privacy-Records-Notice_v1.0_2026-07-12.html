How We Handle Information

Privacy and Records Notice

What this site publishes, what it protects, and who can see what.

The GCSD Culinary Pathway organizes information into three access levels — Public, Participant, and Restricted. This notice explains what each level means, what kind of information belongs in each, and how a page moves between them.

Three Access Levels

PUBLIC

Anyone

Discovery, explanation, recruitment, and approved accountability information — published on the public Culinary Pathway website.

PARTICIPANT

Students, families, mentors, staff

Role-specific handbooks, instructions, forms, and support materials for people currently participating in the program.

RESTRICTED

Authorized personnel only

Student records, Passport data, partner reviews, site visits, incidents, corrective actions, evaluation data, and authorization records. Never placed on the public website.

A document may only move to a wider audience through an explicit revision that removes information inappropriate for that wider audience — it is never assumed safe by default. This rule governs every page and document produced for the Culinary Pathway, not just this site.

What This Public Site Collects

At this planning stage, this site does not operate a live, monitored submission system. The Contact, Help, and Concerns page describes intended future contact routes using clearly labeled placeholders; no personal information submitted through a placeholder is currently received, stored, or acted upon by anyone.

When a real submission method is put in place, this notice will be revised to state plainly what information is collected, why, how long it is kept, and who can see it, consistent with district policy and applicable student privacy law.

Student and Competency Records

Individual student information — including any future Culinary Passport competency record, Gateway progress, attendance, evaluation, or workplace-learning documentation — is treated as a Restricted record. It is never published on this public website. Where a future Participant Resource Center provides students, families, mentors, or staff with access to their own records or role-specific materials, that access will be role-gated and separate from this public site.

Employer and Partner Information

Information about specific employer partners, training stations, or site visits is treated as Restricted. This public site may describe the general idea of employer partnership (see the Contact, Help, and Concerns page) without publishing any specific partner's records, evaluations, or agreements.

Questions About This Notice

Use the General Inquiry route on the Contact, Help, and Concerns page for questions about how information is or will be handled. That route is currently a placeholder pending a real, monitored contact — do not send sensitive information through it.
