Resource boundaries

What can and cannot be public

Public tools

General information, blank student tools, family handouts, employer overviews, and blank event-planning tools may be opened for review.

Proposed WBL tools

Blank proposed senior WBL resources may be reviewed, but they are not active forms and do not approve participation.

Restricted records

Completed Passport records, student records, employer evaluations, hours logs, agreements, site reviews, incident reports, and partner records must stay in approved restricted systems.
