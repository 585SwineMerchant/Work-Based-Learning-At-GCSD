Use the right channel

Public information and blank tools

General information, blank student tools, family handouts, employer overviews, and blank planning resources can be opened, printed, or downloaded now.

Proposed senior WBL review copies

These documents show how a future course could work. They are not active applications, agreements, placements, or official records and must not be submitted through this website.

Restricted records

Completed Passport records, identifiable student work, evaluations, hours logs, agreements, site reviews, incident reports, and partner records must remain in approved restricted systems.
