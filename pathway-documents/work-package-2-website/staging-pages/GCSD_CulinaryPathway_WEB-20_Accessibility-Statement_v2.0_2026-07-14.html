Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

GCSD pathway information should be usable by keyboard, readable on small screens, and available in alternate formats through district channels.

Public commitment

Usable information

The pages use semantic headings, landmarks, keyboard-operable navigation, visible focus styles, responsive layouts, and text labels that do not rely on color alone.

A formal accessibility audit should be completed before live publication.

Need help

Alternate formats

Students, families, staff, and community members who need help accessing information should use the district's accessibility or building support channels.
