Built on an existing Culinary Arts program

GCSD Culinary Pathway

Clear direction from classroom kitchen to career-ready practice.

Greece Central School District already offers Culinary Arts instruction, student enterprise experiences, credential opportunities, and college-credit opportunities. This site shows how current courses, leadership work, authentic production, and a proposed senior work-based learning course can connect into one clearer pathway.

Program foundation

Not starting from zero

Existing courses and enterprise

Culinary Arts and Nutrition I and II, Advanced Culinary Arts, Kitchen Management, and The Cottage give students current places to build culinary skill, professional habits, and authentic production experience.

Proposed organizing framework

The six Gateways and Culinary Passport would organize evidence already produced through coursework, assessment, lab performance, and authentic work so students, families, and teachers can see progress clearly.

Developed senior WBL proposal

A senior external work-based learning course has drafted planning materials and would require district authorization, staffing, legal/risk review, placement procedures, and approved records systems before use.
