Results and Accountability

The program can examine student learning, learning environments, and program quality without creating busywork or exposing private records.

Student learning

What students are actually gaining

Course achievement

Grades, completed assignments, assessed labs, and successful course completion show whether students are meeting course expectations.

Culinary and employability skills

Assessed culinary skills, professional habits, authentic performance, reflection, readiness, and credential attainment show what students can do.

Progression

Student movement through available courses, enterprise roles, leadership opportunities, credentials, college credit, and proposed WBL readiness can show whether the pathway is coherent.

Learning environment quality

Facilities, enterprise work, and partner settings

Kitchen and equipment capacity

Review should consider safety, sanitation, equipment, production capacity, storage, prep space, instructional space, enrollment growth, and whether the current classroom kitchen can meet program needs.

Cottage balance

The Cottage should remain curriculum-driven and student-centered while still giving students authentic client expectations, schedules, quality standards, and service pressure.

Future WBL settings

Future partner sites would need appropriate supervision, safe learning conditions, and professional expectations that match what the program expects students to bring.

Program quality

Useful, inclusive, efficient

Access and inclusion

The program should avoid unnecessary cost barriers, cognitive barriers, and participation barriers while providing meaningful roles for students with varied strengths and needs.

Coherent opportunities

Course sequence, credentials, college credit, enterprise work, leadership, and industry alignment should connect clearly instead of competing for student and teacher time.

Efficiency

Staff time and program resources should produce meaningful student benefit. Documentation should support instruction, safety, continuity, and decisions rather than create busywork.

Public updates

Meaningful measures only

Future public updates could highlight a small number of meaningful measures using aggregated and de-identified information: course offerings, participation, credential opportunities, enterprise activity, major program improvements, and new approved opportunities.

This page does not create an annual reporting obligation.
