Student Role Assignment

Intended user: Students and teachers planning a culinary event or production cycle

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Assign clear responsibilities that match readiness, preparation, communication expectations, safety and quality checkpoints, teacher verification, and role rotation.

When to use: Complete after event scope and tasks are clear and before the team briefing. Review assignments with students and revise when readiness or event needs change.

Team briefing

  • The team can state the event goal and service deadline.
  • Each student can explain the assigned role, definition of done, and check-in points.
  • Safety, sanitation, allergen, equipment, and quality expectations are clear.
  • Students know which decisions stay with the teacher and when to escalate a problem.
  • Communication and handoff routes are clear.
