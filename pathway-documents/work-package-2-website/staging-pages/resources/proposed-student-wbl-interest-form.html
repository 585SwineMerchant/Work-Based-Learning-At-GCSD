Proposed Senior WBL Student Interest Review

Intended user: Students, families, counselors, and Culinary Arts teachers

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Help a student describe career interests, current preparation, possible workplace settings, constraints, and support needs for a future senior work-based learning conversation.

When to use: Use as a review copy only. It does not apply a student to the proposed course, approve readiness, or request a placement.
