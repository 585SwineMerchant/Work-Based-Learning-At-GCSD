Proposed Senior WBL Coordinator Check-In Record

Intended user: A future WBL coordinator conducting a student, mentor, or site check-in

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Document hours, attendance, approved duties, supervision, safety, learning, feedback, supports, concerns, changes, and follow-up without replacing formal incident or change records.

When to use: Use only in an approved restricted system after program authorization. Escalate urgent safety or welfare concerns through the approved district process rather than waiting for this record.
