Proposed Senior WBL Readiness Review

Intended user: Students, Culinary Arts teachers, counselors, and a future WBL coordinator

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Organize evidence that a student has completed relevant coursework and demonstrated the technical, professional, career, and logistical preparation needed before any placement decision.

When to use: Use for review and gap planning only. Completion would support, but never guarantee, future course or placement eligibility.

Proposed senior WBL resource - not yet active

This proposed senior work-based learning resource is not yet active and is not an application for a current placement. No placement is promised. Do not complete or submit this record through the public website. Final use requires district authorization, an approved records system, and confirmed legal, insurance, working-paper, transportation, staffing, supervision, safety, accessibility, and records procedures.

Logistics and governing conditions

  • Possible schedule has been reviewed against academic, family, activity, and employment responsibilities.
  • Transportation remains unresolved until an approved student-specific plan is documented.
  • Accessibility, accommodation, language, medical, and other support needs would use approved school processes.
  • Working papers, permitted hours, duties, equipment, wage status, and other legal conditions would be verified from current official sources.
  • A training station, mentor, duties, supervision plan, agreement, and records workflow would all require approval before launch.

Specific support or additional evidence needed

Identify the gap, responsible support person, target date, and evidence that would show the gap is addressed.

Review recommendation - not a placement decision

  • Continue preparation; substantial gaps remain
  • Re-review after identified evidence or supports are completed
  • Educational readiness evidence appears complete; logistics and authorization review still required
