Proposed Senior WBL Family Information and Planning Review

Intended user: Students, families, counselors, teachers, and a future WBL coordinator

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Explain the proposed course, 300-hour structure, placement conditions, family questions, transportation uncertainty, supervision, records, and future acknowledgment needs without creating active consent.

When to use: Use as a family review copy only. A future authorized course would require district-approved information, acknowledgment, consent, and records procedures.

Proposed senior WBL resource - not yet active

This proposed senior work-based learning resource is not yet active and is not an application for a current placement. No placement is promised. Do not complete or submit this record through the public website. Final use requires district authorization, an approved records system, and confirmed legal, insurance, working-paper, transportation, staffing, supervision, safety, accessibility, and records procedures.

What the proposal would involve

  • A senior external work-based learning course connected to prior Culinary Arts coursework and demonstrated readiness.
  • A proposed total of 300 verified hours across the authorized course calendar. The weekly equivalent cannot be published until the actual calendar and sequence are confirmed.
  • An approved training station, mentor, individualized training plan, duties, schedule, supervision, check-ins, site visits, hours verification, feedback, reflection, portfolio, and final presentation.
  • Student learning before labor and preparation before independence as continuing operating expectations.

What would have to be confirmed before participation

  • District authorization, course title, credit, sequence, staffing, calendar, and student eligibility
  • Training-station screening, agreement, mentor preparation, approved duties/equipment, and supervision
  • Current legal, insurance, labor-law, working-paper, wage/hour, safety, and incident procedures
  • Student-specific transportation, schedule, accessibility, accommodation, and support plans
  • Approved restricted records system, privacy permissions, communication routes, and record owners

What is not promised

  • Enrollment in the proposed course
  • A specific employer, mentor, site, schedule, duty, wage, or transportation arrangement
  • A placement immediately after expressing interest or completing a readiness review
  • That every available site will fit every student's goals, supports, schedule, or transportation needs
