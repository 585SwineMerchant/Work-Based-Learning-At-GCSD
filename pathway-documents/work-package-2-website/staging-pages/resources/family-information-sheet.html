Family Information Sheet

Public information resource.

GCSD Culinary Arts includes current courses, student enterprise activity through The Cottage, credential opportunities, college-credit opportunities, and a proposed framework for organizing readiness evidence.

What families should know

  • Course completion remains required.
  • Credentials and college credit have separate eligibility requirements.
  • The proposed Passport would organize evidence; it would not replace grades.
  • Senior external WBL is a developed proposal, not an active placement course.

GCSD Culinary Pathway resource. Blank proposed senior WBL resources are for review only until approved.
