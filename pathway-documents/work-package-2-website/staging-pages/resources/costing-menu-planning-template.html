Costing and Menu-Planning Template

Intended user: Students and teachers planning a lab, Cottage event, pop-up, or menu project

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Calculate ingredient cost, total recipe cost, cost per serving, and food-cost percentage while documenting yield, allergens, sourcing, and review.

When to use: Use after selecting or testing a recipe and before final purchasing or pricing decisions. The spreadsheet version performs calculations from entered quantities and prices.

Download PDFDownload calculation spreadsheet

Calculation method

For each ingredient, divide purchase price by purchase-unit quantity to find unit cost, then multiply by quantity used. Total the ingredient costs. Cost per serving equals total recipe cost divided by yield. Food-cost percentage equals cost per serving divided by proposed selling or service price, multiplied by 100.
