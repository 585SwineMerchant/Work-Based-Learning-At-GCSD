Proposed Senior WBL Hours Log

Intended user: Students, workplace mentors, and a future WBL coordinator

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Provide a reconciled record of dates, start/end times, breaks, learning activities, student-calculated hours, mentor verification, and cumulative progress toward the proposed 300-hour structure.

When to use: Use only after future launch authorization and through an approved restricted system. No public copy should contain completed student or workplace records.
