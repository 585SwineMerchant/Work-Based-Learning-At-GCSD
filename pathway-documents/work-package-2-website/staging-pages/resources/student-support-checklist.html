Student Support Checklist

Public checklist.

  • Check attendance and punctuality.
  • Review uniform, hygiene, and professional expectations.
  • Ask what skill or habit the student is working on next.
  • Help the student save accurate resume and portfolio evidence.
  • Use teacher/counselor routes for course, credit, or placement questions.
