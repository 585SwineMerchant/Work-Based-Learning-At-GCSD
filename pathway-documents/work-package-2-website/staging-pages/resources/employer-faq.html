Employer FAQ

Public information resource.

Is this employment?

Future WBL would be structured learning, not a staffing solution. Employment, compensation, liability, and agreement details require district decision.

Can partners start small?

Yes. Community connections can begin with guest participation, demonstrations, site visits, feedback, or event support before any placement-level role exists.
