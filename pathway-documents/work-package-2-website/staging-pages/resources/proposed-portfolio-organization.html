Proposed Senior WBL Portfolio Organizer

Intended user: Students, Culinary Arts teachers, and a future WBL coordinator

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Organize common and individualized evidence so the portfolio shows learning, feedback, growth, increasing responsibility, and readiness for a final presentation without becoming a public student record.

When to use: Use as a planning organizer only. Completed student evidence would remain in an approved restricted system and would not be uploaded through the public website.

Evidence quality check

  • Every artifact connects to a course or training-plan objective.
  • Claims are specific, accurate, and supported by evidence.
  • Feedback and resulting improvement are visible.
  • The portfolio shows individual contribution, not only team outcomes.
  • No confidential recipe, customer, personnel, financial, medical, incident, or proprietary information is included without approval.
  • Student and workplace identifiers are limited to what the approved record requires.
  • Files use approved names, formats, access controls, and storage locations.

Missing evidence and recovery plan

Identify the missing category, replacement or equivalent evidence, support person, and target date.

Portfolio story

In two or three sentences, explain the growth this portfolio should make visible to a reviewer.
