Event Reflection

Intended user: Students completing a Cottage, pop-up, capstone, or course event

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Evaluate individual learning, team performance, client-centered work, quality, safety, feedback, and the next improvement.

When to use: Complete after event closeout and before details fade. Answer from your own role, even when the event was a team effort.
