Proposed Senior WBL Employer Feedback

Intended user: A future workplace mentor and WBL coordinator

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Provide structured midpoint or final feedback on performance within approved duties while preserving the coordinator's responsibility for course decisions.

When to use: Complete only through a future approved restricted workflow. Feedback informs student growth and evaluation but does not independently determine readiness, course completion, or Gateway status.
