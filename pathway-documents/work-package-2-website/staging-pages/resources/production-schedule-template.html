Production Schedule

Intended user: Students and teachers planning a culinary lab, Cottage event, pop-up, or capstone

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Turn a service deadline into an ordered production plan with responsibility, dependencies, equipment, holding/storage, quality checks, status, and contingencies.

When to use: Build after the menu and event scope are approved. Update status during production and use the schedule during closeout review.

Pre-service check

  • Every task has an owner and deadline.
  • Dependencies are complete before dependent tasks start.
  • Equipment conflicts and utility needs are resolved.
  • Hot/cold holding and storage locations are confirmed.
  • Quality and safety checkpoints have an assigned reviewer.
  • A contingency exists for late product, equipment failure, ingredient shortage, or staffing change.
