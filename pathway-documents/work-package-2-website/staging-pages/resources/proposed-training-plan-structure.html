Intended user: A future WBL coordinator, student, mentor, family, and Culinary Arts teaching team

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Show how student-specific objectives, approved duties, evidence, schedule, supervision, review points, and governing conditions would be documented before the first counted hour.

When to use: Use for planning and review only. A live plan would require an approved training station, agreement, restricted record, and all required signatures before launch.
