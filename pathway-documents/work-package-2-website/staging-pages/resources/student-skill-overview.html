Student Skill Overview

Public information resource.

Technical skills

  • Food safety, sanitation, personal hygiene, and safe equipment use.
  • Measurement, standardized recipes, mise en place, knife skills, and station organization.
  • Baking, grains, pasta, nutrition, allergens, flavor, kitchen math, costing, and menu planning.
  • Advanced production, client-centered work, quality control, and service expectations.

Employability skills

  • Attendance, punctuality, professional appearance, communication, and teamwork.
  • Resource management, problem solving, ethical/legal responsibility, safety, and quality of work.
  • Leadership, reflection, resume preparation, and career planning as students advance.

Individual readiness depends on course progress, demonstrated performance, and teacher verification.
