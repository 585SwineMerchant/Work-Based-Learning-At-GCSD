Proposed Senior WBL Training-Station Profile

Intended user: A future WBL coordinator and employer/mentor completing a site review

Purpose and directions

Purpose: Capture the operational, educational, supervision, schedule, duty, hazard, accessibility, transportation, and compensation information needed to evaluate one physical work location.

When to use: Complete only through a future approved restricted workflow. One profile would be required for each physical location under review.
