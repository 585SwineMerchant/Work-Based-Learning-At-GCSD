Student questions

A good fit may look like

  • You want hands-on culinary learning with clear safety, teamwork, and professionalism expectations.
  • You are willing to practice, receive feedback, and show readiness before taking on more independence.
  • You are interested in credentials, leadership, enterprise work, hospitality careers, college credit opportunities, or graduation planning connected to CTE.
