Contact, Help, and Concerns

Use monitored school or district channels. Do not submit private student, employer, placement, evaluation, hours, agreement, or incident records through a public webpage.

Monitored channels

Use existing routes

Current student or family

Start with the classroom teacher, school counseling office, or building administrator through normal district communication channels.

Course or schedule question

Use school counseling and the official course-selection process for enrollment, credit, prerequisite, and schedule questions.

Cottage or community inquiry

Use existing school communication channels until a monitored Cottage inquiry route is assigned.

Employer interest

Use the public employer resource page for current boundaries; a low-risk interest route should wait for assigned ownership and privacy review.

Do not use this site

Urgent and sensitive concerns

Emergency and safety concerns: use existing district, school, emergency, or mandated-reporting procedures. A pathway webpage is not an emergency contact route.
  • Do not submit student records or private student details through public pages.
  • Do not submit employer agreements, training plans, site reviews, evaluations, hours, or incident details through public pages.
  • Ask for an accessible alternative through existing district channels until a monitored accessibility route is assigned.
