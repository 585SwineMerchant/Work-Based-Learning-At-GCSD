Graduation and readiness

Outcomes to understand carefully

Credential opportunities

ServSafe is a current credentialing option; other assessment and recognition claims need current confirmation before exact public wording.

Graduation planning

The district pathway presentation connects Culinary Arts to national assessment, CTE endorsement, and possible 4+1 planning. Exact student eligibility remains individually reviewed.

College credit

College-credit opportunities may be part of the pathway, but exact MCC course mappings should not be published until the current articulation is verified.
