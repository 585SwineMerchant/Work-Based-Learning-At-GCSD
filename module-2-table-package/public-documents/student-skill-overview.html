What GCSD Culinary Students Bring to Your Worksite

A Skill Overview for Employer Partners

Students move through a five-course pathway — Strategies for Success, Culinary 1, Culinary 2, Advanced Culinary, and Kitchen Management — building toward ServSafe food safety certification and the YouScience Culinary 3 industry credential, in a program aligned with the National Restaurant Association's ProStart curriculum. Here's what that means for you.

Technical Culinary Skills

  • Knife cuts & equipment — batonnet, julienne, brunoise, dice; safe use of standard kitchen and service equipment
  • Sanitation & food safety — HACCP principles, FIFO rotation, FAT TOM conditions, Big 8 allergen awareness
  • Culinary math — measurement conversion, recipe scaling, food costing
  • Menu planning — concept, costing, and production planning
  • Eggs & dairy — preparation and cookery techniques
  • Produce prep — mise en place and fruit/vegetable fabrication
  • Grains, pasta, potatoes & legumes — core starch preparation
  • Poultry, meat & seafood — fabrication and cookery methods
  • Bakery production — baking and pastry fundamentals

Employability Skills (11 CDOS)

  • Attendance — shows up consistently and reliably
  • Punctuality — arrives and completes tasks on time
  • Workplace appearance — dresses and presents professionally
  • Quality of work — meets standards for accuracy and care
  • Resource management — uses time, ingredients, and equipment efficiently
  • Communication — gives and receives instructions clearly
  • Thinking / problem solving — troubleshoots and adapts under pressure
  • Ethical / legal responsibilities — follows food safety law and workplace conduct standards
  • Interpersonal dynamics — works as part of a kitchen team
  • Foundation development — builds transferable academic and technical skills
  • Safety — follows safe food handling and kitchen safety practices
Before any worksite involvement, students already apply these skills in real conditions — planning, costing, preparing, and serving food for in-house catering and school events under classroom supervision.
