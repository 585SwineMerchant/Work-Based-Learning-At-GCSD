Production Schedule Template

Culinary Pathway · Greece Central School District

A production schedule keeps an SBE event on time by breaking the day into mise en place, production, and service/cleanup — and by naming who is responsible for each task. Fill in tasks and assigned students as the event is planned; update Status as the day unfolds.

Building the schedule around clear, discrete tasks — rather than one long undivided list — makes it easier to rotate roles so students with different strengths, needs, 504 plans, or IEPs can each take ownership of a task that fits them.

This is a draft planning template created as part of a graduate course project (SUNY Oswego, CTE 566). It describes a proposed program and is not an official Greece Central School District communication. Final production scheduling and supervision procedures would be determined by district approval and would need to follow Greece Central School District policy and applicable health/safety requirements.
