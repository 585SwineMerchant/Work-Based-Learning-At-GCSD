Event Planning Checklist

Culinary Pathway · Greece Central School District

Use this checklist to coordinate an SBE event from start to finish. Work through each timeframe with students so planning, production, and reflection responsibilities are visible and shared — not held by one person.

2+ Weeks Before

Menu finalized Market order form submitted Student roles assigned Event Request Form approved Costing/menu pricing reviewed Allergen/dietary plan drafted

1 Week Before

Ingredients confirmed / ordered Equipment checked and reserved Recipes finalized and scaled Signage/marketing materials prepared Serving & packaging supplies confirmed Production schedule drafted

Day-Of

Mise en place complete Sanitation & food safety checks complete FIFO followed on all stored ingredients Uniforms / workplace appearance checked Service area set up and ready Team briefing / role review complete

After

Cleanup complete, stations reset Leftover food handled per protocol Customer feedback collected Event Reflection Form scheduled Photos/documentation submitted, if approved Costing reviewed against actuals
