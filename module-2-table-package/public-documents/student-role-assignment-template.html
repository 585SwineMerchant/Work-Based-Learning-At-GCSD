Student Role Assignment Template

Culinary Pathway · Greece Central School District

A successful SBE event depends on many different roles working together. Assigning and rotating these roles helps every student contribute in a way that matches their strengths — and gives students with different strengths, needs, 504 plans, or IEPs a meaningful, visible way to participate alongside their peers.

Consider rotating roles across events so students build experience in more than one area over the course of the pathway — production, service, and behind-the-scenes roles like costing and marketing are all valuable, resume-ready experience.

This is a draft planning template created as part of a graduate course project (SUNY Oswego, CTE 566). It describes a proposed program and is not an official Greece Central School District communication. Final role-assignment and supervision procedures would be determined by district approval and would need to follow Greece Central School District policy and applicable health/safety requirements.
