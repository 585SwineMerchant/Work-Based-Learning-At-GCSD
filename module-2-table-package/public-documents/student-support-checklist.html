Student Support Checklist

Greece Culinary Work-Based Learning

Families play an important role in helping students succeed in Work-Based Learning. Use this printable checklist as a simple reminder of ways to stay involved — check off items as you go, or revisit them throughout the year.

Ways Families Can Support Students

Discussing career goals and interests
Encouraging strong attendance and punctuality
Helping students practice professional communication
Reviewing expectations before any WBL experience
Encouraging students to ask questions and accept feedback
Helping students reflect on what they are learning
Communicating concerns to school staff promptly
Reinforcing safety, responsibility, and professionalism

Notes

Use this space to jot down questions or concerns to bring up with school staff.
