Mock Interview Checklist

Culinary Pathway · Greece Central School District

Use this checklist to get ready for a mock interview with a culinary teacher, WBL coordinator, or visiting employer partner. Mock interviews are practice — the goal is to build comfort before a real one.

Before the Interview

Reviewed the job or program description Printed or emailed a copy of my resume Practiced answers to common questions out loud Prepared 2–3 questions to ask the interviewer Planned professional, kitchen-appropriate attire Know the date, time, and location (or video link)

Day-Of Reminders

Arrive (or log on) 10 minutes early Bring a printed resume and a pen Offer a firm handshake and eye contact Silence phone before starting Thank the interviewer at the end

Sample Questions to Practice

  • Tell me about yourself and why you're interested in culinary work.
  • Describe a time you worked as part of a team under pressure.
  • How do you handle a mistake you made during service or prep?
  • What food safety practices are you familiar with?
  • Why do you want to work in this kitchen or this type of food service?
  • How do you handle a difficult customer or coworker?
  • Where do you see yourself using these skills in the future?
