Career Goal Reflection Sheet

Culinary Pathway · Greece Central School District

Use this worksheet to think through career interests and goals before or after a Work-Based Learning experience. There are no wrong answers — goals can and will change as students learn more about themselves and the field.

What draws you to culinary or food service work?

Which parts of culinary work have you enjoyed most so far?

Consider: prep, baking/pastry, plating, service, catering, kitchen management, menu planning, customer interaction.

What is one skill you'd like to get better at this year?

Where do you see yourself using these skills in 1–2 years? In 5–10 years?

What is one step you could take this semester to move toward that goal?

For example: earn a certification, ask for a specific WBL experience, talk to a guest chef, or research a program/school.

Career goals are allowed to change. This worksheet is meant to capture where a student's thinking is right now — revisiting it later in the year (or after a new WBL experience) is a good way to see how interests have grown or shifted.

This is a draft reflective worksheet created as part of a graduate course project (SUNY Oswego, CTE 566). It describes a proposed program and is not an official Greece Central School District communication. Final worksheet format and any counseling/advisement use would be determined by district approval.
