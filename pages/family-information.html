Family Information

This page is designed to help families understand what a proposed Culinary Pathway Work-Based Learning experience may involve, and how the school intends to keep families informed at every step.

Course project note: Specific consent, supervision, and communication procedures shown here are proposed concepts for illustration. Final family-facing policies and forms would be determined by district approval.

How the school supports students

Across all proposed Work-Based Learning experiences, the school is designed to maintain responsibility for:

  • Communication with families about student participation and expectations
  • Documentation of hours, learning objectives, and evaluations
  • Supervision, whether on school grounds or at a partner site
  • Safety protocols and site expectations
  • Alignment of experiences to course standards and learning goals
