Forms & Resources: Tools for Students, Families, Employers, and Program Planning

A proposed resource hub for Culinary Work-Based Learning information, forms, flyers, planning tools, and presentation materials.

This page is designed as the central resource hub for the proposed Greece Culinary Work-Based Learning program. In a completed version of the site, students, families, employers, school staff, and community partners could access information sheets, interest forms, readiness tools, reflection logs, employer materials, presentation resources, and evaluation tools from one QR-accessible location. Many of these tools are designed to formalize and document experiences the Culinary Pathway already provides — such as in-house catering, guest chef visits, and advisory board partnerships — connecting those internal experiences to external partnerships over time.

Because this is a graduate course project draft, the resources below are presented as proposed or future materials. Final forms, procedures, and approval processes would need to follow Greece Central School District policies and applicable New York State requirements.

Course project note: No official forms currently exist for this proposed program. The items below are placeholders showing where real documents (PDFs, flyers, permission forms) will be linked once developed and approved.
